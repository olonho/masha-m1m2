## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This briefing synthesizes the current literature (2020–2026) on how macrophage polarization states influence the fate of disseminated tumor cells (DTCs) at metastatic sites. The field has undergone a significant paradigm shift: the classical M1/M2 dichotomy is now recognized as inadequate to capture the full spectrum of macrophage functional states that regulate tumor cell dormancy and reactivation.

## SECTION 1: KEY PAPERS (2020–2026)

### 1.1 Landmark Studies

**Paper 1 — Dalla et al. (2024)** "Lung-resident alveolar macrophages regulate the timing of breast cancer metastasis." *Cell*, 2024. (77 citations)
- Alveolar macrophages maintain DTC dormancy; AM depletion causes dormant DCCs to proliferate.

**Paper 2 — Pereira, Panier, Nater et al. (2025)** "Inflammatory cytokines mediate the induction of and awakening from metastatic dormancy." *Cell Reports*, 2025. (15 citations)
- IFN-gamma directly induces dormancy via high p38/ERK ratio; IL-17A from CD4+ T cells is the essential wake-up signal.

**Paper 3 — Borriello, Coste, Traub et al. (2022)** "Primary tumor associated macrophages activate programs of invasion and dormancy in disseminating tumor cells." *Nature Communications*, 2022. (170 citations)
- Primary tumor macrophages activate BOTH invasion AND dormancy programs simultaneously.

**Paper 4 — Ganesan, Bhasin et al. (2023)** "Taxane chemotherapy induces stromal injury that leads to breast cancer dormancy escape." *PLOS Biology*, 2023. (30 citations)
- Docetaxel injures bone marrow stromal cells, releasing IL-6/G-CSF, driving dormant cell outgrowth.

**Paper 5 — Zhang, Ding, Gu et al. (2025)** "Macrophage regulation of tumor cell dormancy and the dormant niche." *Cancer Metastasis Reviews*, 2025. (4 citations)
- TAMs regulate proliferation-dormancy switch via TGF-beta, WNT, and HIPPO signaling.

**Paper 6 — Chernosky & Tamagno (2021)** "The role of the innate immune system in cancer dormancy and relapse." *Cancers*, 2021. (33 citations)

**Paper 7 — Manjili, Isbell et al. (2022)** "Multifaceted functions of chronic inflammation in regulating tumor dormancy and relapse." *Seminars in Cancer Biology*, 2022. (58 citations)

**Paper 8 — Liang, Chen, Zhang et al. (2026)** "Remodeling the tumor dormancy ecosystem to prevent recurrence and metastasis." *Signal Transduction and Targeted Therapy*, 2026. (7 citations)

## SECTION 2: MECHANISTIC INSIGHTS

### 2.1 Cytokine Signaling Networks

- **TGF-beta** (dominant dormancy inducer): TGF-beta2 through TGF-beta-RIII activates p38alpha/beta
- **IFN-gamma** (dominant dormancy inducer): Activates STAT1, shifts ERK/p38 toward high p38
- **IL-17A** (dormancy exit signal): From CD4+ T cells, shifts p38/ERK toward high ERK
- **IL-6** (reactivation driver): JAK2/STAT3, promotes cell cycle re-entry
- **TNF-alpha** (context-dependent): Dual role depending on concentration and duration
- **IL-10** (M2-associated, immunosuppressive): Creates immune-permissive environment for DTC survival
- **CXCL10/CXCR3 axis** (dormancy maintenance): Sustains immunological dormancy

### 2.2 Key Transcription Factor Pathways

- **STAT3** (reactivation): IL-6/JAK2/STAT3 drives cyclin D1, c-Myc, Bcl-2
- **NF-kappaB** (context-dependent): Acute = apoptosis of DTCs; chronic = survival/angiogenesis
- **NR2F1** (master dormancy regulator): Ligand-activated; agonist C26 activates dormancy programs

### 2.3 ERK/p38 MAPK Balance

- **Low ERK / High p38 = Dormancy**: TGF-beta2, IFN-gamma activate p38, suppress ERK
- **High ERK / Low p38 = Proliferation**: IL-6, IL-17A, EGF activate ERK, suppress p38
- **alpha5beta1 integrin**: Binding to fibronectin activates FAK-ERK; promotes dormancy exit

### 2.4 Angiogenic Switch

M2 macrophage-derived VEGF, bFGF, MMP-9; reduced TSP-1 triggers transition from avascular dormancy to angiogenic outgrowth.

## SECTION 3: DORMANCY MODELS

### 3.1 In Vitro Models

- **D2.0R/D2A1**: Gold standard dormancy-prone mammary tumor cell system
- **Hydrogel-based models**: Tunable ECM properties for dormancy studies
- **Bone marrow stromal co-culture**: Prostate cancer cells with osteoblasts

### 3.2 In Vivo Models

- **Tail vein injection / lung metastasis**: D2.0R tracking in lung tissue
- **TetO-Her2/Neu model**: Doxycycline-regulated Her2/Neu for temporal control
- **PyMT spontaneous metastasis**: Immunocompetent mammary tumor model
- **Bone metastasis models**: Intracardiac injection for bone colonization

### 3.3 Patient-Derived Models

- **HNSCC PDX**: Aguirre-Ghiso lab, macroH2A enrichment in dormant DCCs
- **Patient bone marrow DTC analysis**: NR2F1 stratification of dormant DTCs

## SECTION 4: OPEN QUESTIONS AND CONTROVERSIES

1. **Inadequacy of M1/M2 dichotomy**: scRNA-seq reveals spectrum; co-expression of M1/M2 markers common
2. **Active vs. passive dormancy maintenance**: IFN-gamma required for induction but maintenance is T-cell independent
3. **Therapeutic maintenance vs. forced awakening**: Risk-benefit of each strategy
4. **Chemotherapy-induced dormancy escape**: Ganesan et al. (2023) taxane paradox
5. **Macrophage plasticity in response to therapy**: Lacks prospective clinical studies

---

## SECTION 5: KEY DATA POINTS

- **NR2F1**: Agonist C26 induces self-regulated increase in NR2F1 mRNA and protein (Khalil et al., 2022)
- **MacroH2A**: Enriched in solitary dormant DCCs in HNSCC PDX (Sun et al., 2022)
- **Ki67**: Dormant DCCs uniformly Ki67-negative
- **p-p38/p-ERK ratio**: Established biochemical determinant of dormancy
- **Pereira et al. (2025)**: CD4+ T cell-deficient mice fail to exit dormancy, reduced metastatic burden
- **Dalla et al. (2024)**: AM depletion increases proliferation of dormant DCCs
- **Ganesan et al. (2023)**: Docetaxel increases M2 macrophages and IL-6/G-CSF in bone marrow

---

## CONCLUSIONS AND STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

1. M1/M2 framework is a starting point, not an endpoint
2. Tissue context is paramount — alveolar, bone marrow, Kupffer, microglia each distinct
3. Temporal dynamics are undersampled — need longitudinal in vivo studies
4. Therapeutic opportunity at dormancy-maintenance stage: IFN-gamma/IL-17A/CXCL10 axes
5. Clinical translation is the bottleneck — adjuvant studies needed
